The WonderTax Labs Brand

WonderTax Labs stands for integrity, transparency, and professionalism. We value and respect our team members, platform partners, and customers. We take pride in our accountability and teamwork, believing "teamwork makes the dream work."

This document explains how best to use our WonderTax Labs brand name and logo.

We encourage you to read this document carefully before using our brand name or logo. Your cooperation is highly appreciated!

The WonderTax Labs Word Mark

  1. "WonderTax Labs" should be used solely to refer to our company.
  2. "WonderTax Labs" is a singular entity. Although the word mark ends with an "s" (suggesting plural), it is still singular.
  3. WonderTax Labs has a registered United States Trademark on the word "WonderTax" (registration number: 90056834). As such, it must include the Registered Trademark symbol (®) upon first use in any publication to sufficiently put the public on notice of our registered trademark. The registered trademark symbol does not need to be repeated in subsequent use in the same publication.
  4. If possible, we prefer the ® symbol to be set in superscript (in HTML: <sup>&reg;</sup>).
  5. Our registered trademark is on the term "WonderTax" — not on the longer "WonderTax Labs"
    • Correct: WonderTax® Labs
    • Acceptable: WonderTax® Labs
    • Incorrect: WonderTax Labs®
